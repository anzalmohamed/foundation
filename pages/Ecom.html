ECONOMIC EMPOWERMENT –(SUNRISE SERVICES LIMITED)

For economy to growth, any country MUST increase in its capacity to produce goods and services within a specific period. Economic growth is a long-term expansion in the productive potential of the economy to satisfy the wants of individuals in the society. Sustained economic growth of a country’ has a positive impact on the national income and level of employment, which further results in higher living standards. For poverty to be eradicated, all citizens must focus on production; when each one of us can produce what he/she needs, poverty will be history and when we can produce more than our needs, then goods and services can be exported which is a good economic indicator
