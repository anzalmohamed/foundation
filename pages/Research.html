SCIENCE & RESEARCH -GLOBAL ISLAMIC RESEARCH FOUNDATION – GIRF

The main purpose of research is to inform action, to prove a theory, and contribute to developing knowledge in a field or study. Significance of research;

  1. A Tool for Building Knowledge and for Facilitating Learning
  2. Means to Understand Various Issues and Increase Public Awareness
  3. An Aid to Business Success
  4. A Way to Prove Lies and to Support Truths
  5. Means to Find, Gauge, and Seize Opportunities
  6. A Seed to Love Reading, Writing, Analyzing, and Sharing Valuable Information
  7. Nourishment and Exercise for the Mind
  8. Science is the study of the nature and behavior of natural things and the knowledge that we obtain about them. Our approach in all our projects is based on religion, philosophy and science

DFI’s research desk engages in the following;

  1. Establishment of research Centers based on religious & scientific investigations
  2. Delivery of topical lectures at home and abroad on various topical issues based on religion, philosophy and science
  3. Training of research students
  4. Conduct thematic scientific congresses

TECHNOLOGY -KENYA INSITUTE OF APPLIED FINANCIALS & MANAGEMENT (KIFAM)

Technology is science or knowledge put into practical use to solve problems or invent useful tools.

If you’re ready to graduate and start your career now, consider how long it will take to complete your program. Many students at four-year universities take longer than four years to graduate – and in fact, on average, spend six years earning their degree! At a technical school, on the other hand, you can graduate in as little as one year and make anywhere from $30,000 to $60,000 per year – and even more after a few years in your field.

KIFAM KAMKUNJI is a technical College in Nairobi Kenya that offers Technical Islamic Integrated program. Primary Schools graduates with a pass in their 5 subjects are eligible for admission at age 14. they pursue courses that will lead to simultaneous degrees; one in Islamic Religious Education and another in Business related course, Engineering or Health Sciences
